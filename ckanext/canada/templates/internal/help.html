    {{ _("Why can't I create an account?") }}

    2. {{ _('Enter your Username, Example: djohn, and click "Request Reset" button.') }}

    {{ _('How do I search the Registry?') }}

    {{ _('Use Search to find metadata records published in the Registry. This feature is the same as Advanced Search on the Open Government Portal.
